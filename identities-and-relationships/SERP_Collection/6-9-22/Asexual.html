          Asexuality, defined
          Sexual attraction, in basic terms, means you find a specific person sexually appealing and want to have sex with them. Asexual people, who might use the term ace or aces for short, typically don't experience sexual attraction or want to pursue sexual relationships with other people.          Nov 25, 2019
